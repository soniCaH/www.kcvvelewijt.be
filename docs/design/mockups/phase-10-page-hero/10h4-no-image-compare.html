Trade-offs

  • n1 typographic — honest + calm; the headline becomes the hero, no faux-filler. Scales perfectly to compact. Risk: a no-lead bare page is still quiet (but that's arguably correct for /scheurkalender).
  • n2 JerseyShirt — keeps the split rhythm + adds the brand illustration. Risk: a jersey on /kalender or a CMS page can read as random/off-topic (JerseyShirt "means" player/team elsewhere).
  • n3 ornament — adds paper character + colour pop without claiming to be a photo. Risk: an oversized quote glyph is decorative filler; needs a motif that doesn't feel arbitrary.
