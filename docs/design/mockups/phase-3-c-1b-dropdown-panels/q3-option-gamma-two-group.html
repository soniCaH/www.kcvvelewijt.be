Distribution: 6 / 5
Wie we zijn (6) — Geschiedenis · Organigram · Bestuur · Jeugdbestuur · KCVV Angels · KCVV Ultras
Praktisch (5) — Praktische Info · Word vrijwilliger · Cashless clubkaart · Contact · Downloads

Reads as: a 2-page spread. Left page = identity (who we are), right page = everything practical (what you can do). Cleanest distribution of the three options — both columns are visually balanced. Reads less like an index and more like a functional split.

Pros

  • Cleanest visual balance: 6 vs 5 reads as nearly symmetrical.
  • No "short orphan column" — both groups feel substantial.
  • Wider columns (≈ 240px each) give labels more breathing room.
  • Two groups are easier to scan-and-decide than three.
  • Easier to maintain — one fewer label to author and translate.

Cons

  • "Praktisch" is a heterogeneous bucket — Word vrijwilliger sits next to Downloads, which is conceptually odd.
  • Loses the "Doe mee" jersey-bright accent moment β has — engagement actions are no longer isolated.
  • Compound heading ("Praktisch") is the longest of any option — risks wrapping at 240px col width.
  • Less editorial nuance — the structure looks more utilitarian, less curated.

Distribution profile

  • Col 1: 6 items (full-height)
  • Col 2: 5 items (~⅚ height)
  • Visual rhythm: balanced
  • Each column ≈ 240px wide (more roomy)
  • Panel overall feels like a 2-page spread, not a 3-col index
