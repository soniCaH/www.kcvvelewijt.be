Hover-grace timing — 3 presets, hover to feel

Same locked panel (Q1 ink-fill / Q2 wide / Q3 two-group / Q4 paper-slide / Q5 chevron). Three different sets of grace timings around the hover-open / hover-close cycle. The open-grace debounce delays opening so brief hovers don't trigger. The close-grace delays closing so cursor sweeps don't lose the panel mid-flight.

HOVER: hover the De club nav item in each frame. Sweep your cursor in and out at different speeds to feel the timing differences.
