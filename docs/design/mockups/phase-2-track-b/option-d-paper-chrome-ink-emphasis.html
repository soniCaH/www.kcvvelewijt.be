★ 03 · FILTER TABS — paper-chip body fixed, three active-state options to compare

Drie actieve opties.

Chip body locked: paper-card vocabulary with `border-2 ink` + `shadow-paper-sm`, mono caps label, count after a hairline pipe (`LIVE | 1`). Three options for the active state below — pick one.

VARIANT 1 JERSEY FILL — from Direction A

Active chip flips to bg-jersey text-ink. Loud but unmistakable. Owner concern: green active may pull attention away from editorial.

VARIANT 2 INK INVERT — synthesis proposal ★ GEKOZEN

Active flips to bg-ink text-cream (matches BrandedTabs). Strongest readable contrast, doesn't compete with editorial jersey moments. Multi-active works (each active chip independently inverted).

VARIANT 3 GREEN OUTLINE — from your reference image

Border + count digit flip to jersey-deep, body stays cream. Most restrained. Multi-active reads cleanly. Risk: subtler than other two — at quick glance the active state may not pop on a busy page. Test in cohesion mock to verify.

↑ FOR (across all three)

Paper-card chip body reads as filters at a glance — addresses your B concern. Hairline pipe count divider keeps things readable without a pill stamp. No leading glyphs.

↓ RESOLVED

Variant 2 (ink-invert) chosen. Chrome states are uniformly ink-coded across BrandedTabs + FilterTabs; jersey stays reserved for content moments only. Variants 1 + 3 retained above as historical reference, will not ship.
