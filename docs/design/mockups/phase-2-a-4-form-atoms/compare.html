Recommendation — Direction B, with two small borrowings.

From A: 2px border-thickness swap on focus (stacked under the halo) so focus is unmistakable from across the screen.

From C: nothing structural in the field — but the FOUT paper-pill badge stays as the shared error-row treatment, giving the editorial vocabulary one foothold inside every form.

What we walk away from: Track B chrome resonance and C's strong filled-state anchor. If owner feedback during implementation says "the form feels too floaty / I can't tell what's done", we revisit C's ink/60 filled border as a B+ amendment. We do not need to commit to that upfront.
