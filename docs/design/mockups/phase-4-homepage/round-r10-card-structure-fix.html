What changes structurally.

Property Current (nested) Proposed (flush)
Outer frame TapedCard with `padding="md"` (~16px) TapedCard with `padding="0"` on top + sides; padding ONLY inside meta panel
Inner figure `<TapedFigure>` inside the card · own border + paper-shadow + padding No `<TapedFigure>` inside the card · image fills top region of TapedCard directly
Image positioning Inset, surrounded by 16px of cream + 1px ink border on all four sides Flush to top, left, right edges of the outer card · bounded only by the card's own border
Tape strips On the inner `<TapedFigure>` frame On the outer `<TapedCard>` frame · positioned at top-left + top-right corners, overlapping the image
Image / meta divider The figure's own bottom border + 16px cream padding gap Single 1px ink rule (`border-top` on the meta panel section)
Visual register "Taped polaroid placed on a paper card" · two paper levels "Photo printed directly onto a paper card" · one paper level
