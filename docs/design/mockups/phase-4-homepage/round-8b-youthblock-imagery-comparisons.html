YouthBlock — quieter image directions

Round 8 over-amplified the JerseyShirt. Today's <YouthSection> is actually text-only — no image at all. Below: four quieter image options, each consistent with the retro-fanzine vocabulary.

Locked. Full-bleed jersey-deep section. Cream text. Editorial accent = #f0c264 (warm yellow). Stats hardcoded ("220+ spelers · 16 ploegen"). Divisions = Bovenbouw / Middenbouw / Onderbouw. CTA → /jeugd. This sub-round picks the imagery direction.
N.1
Text only · no image
Closest to today's behaviour. Pure text on solid jersey-deep. No imagery. Quietest and most predictable. Section reads as a "statement" interlude rather than a hero image.
Tradeoffs + No image asset to maintain
+ No fade / overlay concerns
− Can feel flat compared to other body sections
− Loses the "moment" feel
