Phase 4 · Round 6 · ScheduleStandingsBlock layout

Schedule + standings — one block, what shape?

New section in Phase 4 — combines the next several matches with the league standings on a single homepage band. Replaces today's <MatchesSlider> (which only shows matches, no standings). Phase 3 chrome <MatchStrip> still handles the "next match peek" above the hero — this section goes deeper.

Locked from prior rounds. Section sits between bannerSlotA and bannerSlotB on the spine (right after NewsGrid). Data: getNextMatches() from BFF (PSD-sourced) + getRanking(teamId=1235) (new homepage call). KCVV row in standings highlighted by team_id === 1235.
L.1
Side-by-side split
Schedule on left, standings on right. Both visible at once. Mobile stacks schedule above standings. Most data exposed without interaction.
Tradeoffs + Both visible at once
+ No interaction needed
− Doubles horizontal real-estate
− Mobile = tall stacked block
