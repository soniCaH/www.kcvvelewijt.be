WebshopStrip — what fills the 4 thumbnails?

Today's <WebshopSection> is a single static image (/images/jerseys.png) + external link to KCVV's webshop. Phase 4 (per issue spec) becomes 4 thumbnails in a 4-col grid. This round picks what fills each thumbnail and where the data lives.

Locked. 4-col grid (repeat(4, 1fr) on desktop, 2-col on tablet, 1-col on mobile). Each thumb is a clickable Link target — either to a specific webshop product or the webshop home. /webshop external link visible at section bottom in every variant. This sub-round picks the thumbnail content.
W.1
4 jersey photos · hardcoded
4 hardcoded jersey product photos (home / away / training / GK). Each thumb links to its specific product on the external webshop. Photos stored in /public/images/webshop/*.jpg. Closest to issue spec.
Tradeoffs + No editor work
+ Photos are real product imagery
− Must update images each season
− Hardcoded URLs drift if webshop restructures
