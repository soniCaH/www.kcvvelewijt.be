Phase 4.5 · Round R1.5 · Hero per-articleType flourishes

What hangs off each articleType on the hero?

With R1.B locked (single static hero + Uitgelicht row), the hero now renders ONE article at a time — and the visitor sees its full composition rather than a rotating shell. That means per-articleType artefacts read clearly: the interview gets its interviewee chips, the event gets its day-stamp, the transfer gets its directional chip and jersey number. This round locks the composition for all 4 variants side-by-side so they read coherently as a family.

Each variant below shows the SAME shared shell (kicker · headline · lead · taped polaroid right · Lees verder →) with only the per-type artefacts varying. Layout, typography, tape rotation, and shadows are identical across variants — only the highlighted new artefacts differ.

Shared base (locked): 60/40 split (copy left, photo right), italic-serif H1 with optional jersey-deep accent, mono-uppercase kicker row, EditorialLead, jersey-deep "Lees verder →" link, byline, TapedFigure with warm/jersey tape strips. Hero photo is the article's cover image (landscape, no cutout per feedback_subject_photo_fallback).
