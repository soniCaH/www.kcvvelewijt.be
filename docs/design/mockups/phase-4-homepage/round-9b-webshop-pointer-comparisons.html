Webshop block — pointer to external supplier, not a product gallery

Round 9 was wrong premise. KCVV doesn't sell merch directly — the section is a click-through to an external supplier (training gear + limited personalized "club packages"). Audience is primarily youth and parents who need training kit. No specific products to advertise — just the pointer + context.

What's actually true (owner clarification 2026-05-07). No in-site store. Always external link. Use case = youth/parents picking general training gear from the supplier + limited personalized "club packages" (bundled new-player kit). Section needs to: tell visitors the supplier exists, frame the use case, single CTA click-through. Specific product thumbnails are misleading (no stock guarantee, no per-item curation effort).
P.1
Single CTA card · one visual + headline + CTA
Two-column: visual on the left (a single tape-rotated jersey illustration), text + CTA on the right. Honest single-purpose card. Reuses <JerseyShirt> at right scale (small thumbnail-sized). One click-through. Cleanest.
Tradeoffs + Single click-through, no clutter
+ No product stock to maintain
− Doesn't differentiate "training gear" vs "club packages"
− Single visual carries less variety
