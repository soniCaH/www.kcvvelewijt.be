Fixture: Steakfestijn 2026 — 3-day clubfeest event with per-day sessions, address, capacity, ticket CTA, and note. Picked because the sessions schedule (vrijdag 18:00–22:00 / zaterdag 17:00–23:00 / zondag 11:30–15:00) doesn't fit in the hero strip — it's the kind of detail that justifies a body-level block. Simple single-day events with no sessions[] may not need the detail block at all (single-day + single time range is already in the strip).

Past-vs-upcoming behaviour is noted at the end of the page — not a per-option variation but a sub-decision (overlay / badge / full hide) for the locked option.

Sub-decision · Past-vs-upcoming behaviour
What happens to the detail card / CTA after endDate < today?
Separate from the placement choice (A/B/C/D), the locked option needs a rule for past events. The three candidates: overlay ("AFGELOPEN" stamp + greyed detail card, no CTA); badge (small "afgelopen" pill in the card head, CTA hidden); full hide (don't render the detail card at all once the event has passed — the article body stays as historical record).
Sub-options A Overlay (greyed + AFGELOPEN stamp)
B Badge in card head, CTA hidden
C Full hide of the detail card

Pick a body placement first (A/B/C/D); the past-event behaviour is a follow-up sub-decision on the locked option. The lock document will record the chosen past-event rule alongside the placement.
