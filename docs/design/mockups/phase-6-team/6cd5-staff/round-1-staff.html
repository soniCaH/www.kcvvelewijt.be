The staff section.

A small support group: head/assistant/keeper trainer + afgevaardigde. Photo (monogram fallback when absent) + name + function. The function shows the readable PSD title (Hoofdtrainer / Keeperstrainer / …); codes like "T1"/"T2"/"TK" map to readable labels, falling back to the role bucket (Trainer / Afgevaardigde) when functionTitle is empty.
