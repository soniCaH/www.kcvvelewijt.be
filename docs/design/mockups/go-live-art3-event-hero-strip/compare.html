ART-3 · Event-fact "hero strip" — contained & redesigned (#2237)

On event-articleType articles (/nieuws/[slug]) the FIRST eventFact is hoisted out of the body and rendered between the hero and the article text by apps/web/src/components/article/blocks/EventDetailBlock/EventDetailBlock.tsx.

Today that strip is full-bleed, edge-to-edge, container-less and visually weak — a thin grey bar reading SPORTPARK DRIESPUT, ELEWIJT · 19 SEPTEMBER 2026 · 10:00–17:00. The point of this decision: a properly CONTAINED, redesigned event-fact panel (≤ 1040 wide container) so the article reads as an event.

Scope guard: the in-body inline eventFact polaroid (EventFactInline) is locked separately — NOT touched here. Each variant below shows the panel IN CONTEXT (event hero → panel → body prose) and isolates ONE design choice. The dashed green ruler marks the wide container so you can confirm nothing bleeds. Palette: cream/ink + jersey-deep #008755 (never bright jersey), sharp corners only, CTAs Reserveer + Zet in agenda.

A Ticket-stub panel Isolates: a perforated TicketStub with a big ink date block on the left, venue/time as mono rows, jersey-deep CTA.
