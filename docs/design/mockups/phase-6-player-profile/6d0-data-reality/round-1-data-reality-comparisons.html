Phase 6 · Round 1 · Drill 6.d0 · 2026-05-21

Data-reality reconciliation

Master plan §5.3 specifies a 12-item player-profile stack. A data audit against packages/sanity-schemas/src/player.ts and the BFF contract revealed that three sections fabricate content: <CareerLogTable> has no schema field and no source, <RecentMatchesGrid> needs a new BFF endpoint (current squadPlayerStatistics is season-aggregate only), and the NIEUW hero badge needs a join-date field.

Three variants below show what Phase 6.A ships under each scope commitment. Maxim Breugelmans is the canonical subject — same player as docs/design/mockups/retro-terrace-fanzine/player-profile-desktop.png. Pick based on willingness to commit to editorial + BFF work, not visual taste.

Shippable today (no schema/BFF change) Needs Sanity schema + editorial population Needs new BFF endpoint
