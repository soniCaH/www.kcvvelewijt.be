Data sourcing

  • Δ Extend qaBlock Portable Text with a pullquoteCandidate decorator on answer marks
  • Query: ArticleRepository.findRelated(player.id) → pick first article → traverse qaBlocks[] → find answer with decorator
  • Empty fallback: no decorator → render paragraph alone (no PullQuote)

Tradeoff

  • Quote and bio drift independently — quote is "what he said in a recent interview"
  • Privacy: minor players have no interview articles by editorial policy → quote never appears for U18 profiles
  • Renders only when an interview happens; A-team coverage is partial (~30% of squad have a recent interview)

Add a custom pullquote decorator mark to the existing bio Portable Text array. Editor selects a substring when writing the bio; the decorator both marks it inline (highlight) and surfaces it as the right-column PullQuote. Bio and quote are guaranteed coherent.
