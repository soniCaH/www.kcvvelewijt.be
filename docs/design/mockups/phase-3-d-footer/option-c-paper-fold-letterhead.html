↑ FOR

The most paper-native of the four directions — every band sits on the same cream sheet, separated only by hairline ink rules. The fold-shadow at the top is a literal 2px gradient, not a chrome trick. Reads as continuous with the body content above; the page does not "end with a band of black", it folds.

↓ AGAINST

Quietest of the four. No ink-fill emphasis, no jersey-bg interlude — relies entirely on typography and rules to create hierarchy. Risk: at long scroll, the visual end-of-page signal is weaker than an ink-bg footer. Mitigation: the 2px fold-gradient + 1px ink top rule together provide a deliberate edge.

§ 02 · SECTIONS BREAKDOWN — per-band rationale and tradeoffs

Vier vouwen, vier banden.

The footer reads top-to-bottom as four logical bands: letterhead, directory, motto, colofon. Each band has its own rationale and its own per-band tradeoffs — listed below.

Band 01 · Letterhead

Crest left (96×96 ink-bordered cream square with display-italic K), address block right. Kicker mono row above headline ("K. F. C. V. V. ELEWIJT § STAMNUMMER 5481 * OPGERICHT 1942"). Headline reads "Koninklijke Football Club Voorwaarts Elewijt." in display-900 with the italic accent on the brand-defining word. Below: 4-column dl of address / phone / email / kantine, each cell separated by a 1px paper-edge rule. The headline is the editorial anchor — everything else in the footer subordinates to it.

↑ FOR

Establishes the "this is a printed letter" reading immediately. The crest box is a known primitive (TicketStub geometry). Address dl is information-dense without feeling like a form.

↓ AGAINST

4-column dl can wrap awkwardly at narrow viewports. Mitigation: stack to 2×2 below 768px (production responsive rule, not shown in this mockup).

Band 02 · Directory

3 columns CLUB / TEAMS / VOLG separated by 1px paper-edge vertical rules. Each column heading is mono-caps with a coloured typographic glyph (§ * +) prefix, underlined by 1px ink rule. Link rows use 1px dotted paper-edge rules between items. Press-down hover canon: translate(1px,1px) + jersey-deep recolour on hover. No shadow shift because the rows have no shadow — the haptic tap reading comes from the 1px translate alone.

↑ FOR

Symmetric 3-column grid is conventional and parseable. Hairline rules keep the eye moving without ink weight. Glyph-prefixed headings reuse the typographic vocabulary established in motto + kicker — no new icon system introduced for the footer.

↓ AGAINST

Press-down without a shadow change is a softer haptic than the canonical shadow→none press; the link still re-tints to jersey-deep, which carries the weight of the hover signal. Owner sign-off requested on whether this softer press is acceptable in the letterhead's quiet register.

Band 03 · Motto

Centered, no background, no border. Two typographic ornament rows of § * + * § alternating jersey-deep/ink. Display-italic 700 "Er is maar één" line, then display-900 caps "PLEZANTE COMPAGNIE" with the italic accent on COMPAGNIE for editorial rhythm. Mono-caps signature line "— Sinds 1942 · Elewijt · België —" beneath. Verbatim motto: "ER IS MAAR ÉÉN PLEZANTE COMPAGNIE".

↑ FOR

Hand-set typography earns a moment in the page without bg or chrome. The italic-on-caps split echoes the EditorialHeading vocabulary. Ornaments use only the three approved typographic glyphs (§ * +) — no Lucide, no Stenciletta.

↓ AGAINST

Display-900 caps at clamp(36px,4.6vw,56px) is the tallest type in the footer; on very narrow viewports the line can break awkwardly between PLEZANTE and COMPAGNIE. Acceptable break — both halves are scannable in isolation.

Band 04 · Colofon

Three-column mono-caps strip: stamnummer + BTW left, copyright center (with bolded ink-coloured KCVV ELEWIJT), legal links right (Privacy · Cookies · Colofon). All 10.5px IBM Plex Mono, 0.12em tracking. Sits flush below the motto's bottom rule with 22px top padding — quiet sign-off, no border, no fill.

↑ FOR

Legal/admin info compressed into one mono-caps line — no second band of chrome. The bold KCVV ELEWIJT in copyright doubles as a quiet wordmark sign-off. Press-down hover canon on the legal links keeps the interactive vocabulary consistent.

↓ AGAINST

Three-cell row at narrow widths needs to stack. At ≤640px production rule should be: stack center → left → right, all left-aligned, with 8px gap. Not shown in mockup.

Press-down hover canon — applied surfaces

Two surface classes carry the canonical paper-stamp press idiom: directory link rows and colofon legal links. Both apply transform: translate(1px, 1px) with transition: all 300ms ease and recolour to jersey-deep (directory) or ink (colofon legal). The shadow shift component of the canon is omitted on these surfaces because the rows have no resting shadow — the recolour + translate alone supply the haptic tap reading. Address links (phone, email) carry only an underline-shift on hover — they are inline within body copy, not interactive cards, and the press-down idiom would over-signal at body-text scale.

Responsive behaviour (production rules · not in mockup)

  • ≥1024px — as rendered above. Letterhead 2-col, address dl 4-col, directory 3-col, colofon 3-col
  • 768–1023px — letterhead stays 2-col, address dl drops to 2×2, directory stays 3-col with reduced inner padding (16px), colofon stays 3-col
  • 640–767px — letterhead drops to single column (crest above headline), address dl stacks to 2×2 then 1-col, directory stacks to 1-col with full-width dotted rules between columns (the 1px ink vertical rules drop entirely), colofon stacks center → left → right, all left-aligned
  • ≤639px — outer padding drops to 24px, headline clamps to 28px, motto line B clamps to 32px. The 2px fold-shadow gradient and 1px ink top rule remain unchanged at every breakpoint — the fold metaphor scales with the page

Accessibility

  • Footer carries role="contentinfo" + aria-label="Site footer"
  • Directory carries aria-label="Footer navigatie" on its <nav>
  • External directory links (Facebook, Instagram, X, YouTube) use the ↗ arrow glyph as an aria-hidden visual cue; production wires rel="noopener" + screen-reader-only "(opens in new tab)" text
  • Crest letter is aria-hidden="true" — the wordmark in the headline below carries the accessible club name
  • Motto ornament rows are aria-hidden="false" by default but carry no semantic content; if owner prefers, flip to aria-hidden="true" to prevent SR announcement of "section section asterisk plus asterisk section"
  • Colour contrast: ink (#0a0a0a) on cream (#f5f1e6) ≈ 17.5:1 — well above AAA. ink-muted (#6b6b6b) on cream ≈ 4.9:1 — passes AA for body, fails AAA — acceptable for mono-caps labels and arrows but not for body copy (none used here)
  • Press-down hover canon is supplemented in production by a :focus-visible 2px ink outline — not shown in mockup
