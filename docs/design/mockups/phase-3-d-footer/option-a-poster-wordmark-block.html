★ 02 · SECTIONS BREAKDOWN — each band in isolation, with FOR / AGAINST tradeoffs

Drie banden.

Each band is a distinct surface with a distinct job. Reviewers should be able to push back on one band without rejecting the whole footer — so each appears here on its own with the questions it raises.

BAND 01 · POSTER WORDMARK · jersey-green full-bleed · Freight Display 900 · ER IS MAAR ÉÉN PLEZANTE meta-row

★ ER IS MAAR ÉÉN PLEZANTE · STAMNR. 5503 · OPGERICHT 1928

KCVV Elewijt

★ COMPAGNIE ★   Voetbalstraat 1A · 1982 Elewijt

↑ FOR

Wordmark scale claims the page-end the way a stadium banner claims a wall. The mono motto row anchors the brand voice ("er is maar één plezante") in the canonical phrasing without burning a Playfair line on it. Jersey-deep + cream is the highest-contrast pair in the token map — readable, unambiguous, brand-locked.

↓ AGAINST

A 188px wordmark on every page is loud — at the bottom of a quiet article it can feel like a second hero. Italic Elewijt with a soft drop-shadow risks reading as "logo-on-logo" if the page above already shows the SiteHeader logo. Mitigation: keep the band height fixed and resist the temptation to add a crest sprite.

BAND 02 · DIRECTORY BLOCK · cream paper · 3 columns (CLUB / CONTACT / VOLG ONS) · mono labels + italic display headings + dotted-rule list
★ 01 · CLUB

Bestuur, A-ploeg, B-ploeg, jeugdwerking, geschiedenis, lid worden, sponsor worden — primary site-map in italic display heading + plain-link list.

★ 02 · CONTACT

Adresblok in body text (rectangular photo-default doesn't apply — pure text) + clickable e-mail/telefoon/route/uren links. No map embed in this band.

★ 03 · VOLG ONS

Typographic glyph row (FB · IG · X · YT, no Lucide), short paragraph framing the channels, and a paper-card newsletter mini reusing form-atom chrome.

↑ FOR

Cream block lands the user back into the same paper foundation the rest of the page used — no tonal whiplash between the green poster and the legal strip. Three columns is the conventional footer scan-pattern, which lowers cognitive load at end-of-page. The italic display per-column heading carries voice without pushing past CLAUDE.md's "no Stenciletta" rule.

↓ AGAINST

Three columns at 1280px wrap to a single column ≤880px — the band gets tall fast on phones, which can push the ink strip below the fold on short viewports. The newsletter mini lives only in column 3, which makes its discoverability viewport-dependent. Counter-pattern would be hoisting the newsletter into its own dedicated band above the directory.

BAND 03 · INK STRIP · pure ink bg · cream mono caps · copyright left · small links right · jersey star separators
© 2026 KCVV ELEWIJT ★ ALLE RECHTEN VOORBEHOUDEN ★ STAMNR. 5503 PRIVACY · COOKIES · COLOFON · PERS

↑ FOR

Ink strip is the quietest possible legal end-note — no decoration, no display type, just IBM Plex Mono in cream on ink. Jersey-green star separators are the only chromatic accent and they reuse a glyph already in the meta-row above, so the band has zero new vocabulary. Reads like the colophon line at the bottom of a printed match-day programme.

↓ AGAINST

Pure ink fill is a third surface colour for the footer (after green and cream), which means the page closes on three distinct backgrounds in 600px of vertical space. If a future reviewer wants the strip to absorb into the directory block, this band can be removed and copyright/links can move into the bottom row of column 1 — at the cost of reduced legal-link legibility.

★ NOTES — divergence from screenshot, token map, open questions

Resolved

  • Footer is a fresh divergent design — not a refinement of the retro-terrace-fanzine screenshot footer (per project_phase_3_footer_divergence.md)
  • Hero element of the footer is the wordmark, not the directory — the band reads as a poster pasted over the page-end
  • Three bands, three surface colours, one envelope: jersey-deep → cream → ink
  • Motto rendered verbatim as "ER IS MAAR ÉÉN PLEZANTE" — never "meer dan een club" (per reference_club_identity.md)
  • Social glyphs are typographic (* FB *, * IG *, * X *, * YT *), not Lucide icons (per feedback_visual_preferences.md)
  • Newsletter input + submit reuse Phase 2 Track A form-atom chrome verbatim
  • Press-down hover canon: transform: translate(1px, 1px) + transition-all 300ms on every interactive (per feedback_canonical_press_down_hover.md)
  • No tape, no rotation, no border-radius — footer is a flat sibling of header, alerts, and tabs
  • Stenciletta is not reached for — wordmark uses Freight Display 900 (production) / Playfair Display 900 (mockup) per feedback_visual_preferences.md
  • Bottom strip reuses ink + jersey-star pattern from SiteHeader top-strip; the page opens and closes on the same chrome bookend
