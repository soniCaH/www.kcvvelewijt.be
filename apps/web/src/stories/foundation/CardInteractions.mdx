## Green Accent Bar

Navigation cards display a green accent bar that expands from center on hover using a `clip-path` animation. The bar sits at the top of the card and is purely decorative.

**Markup:**

```html
<div
  className="absolute top-0 inset-x-0 h-[3px] bg-kcvv-green-bright z-20
    pointer-events-none [clip-path:inset(0_50%)]
    group-hover:[clip-path:inset(0_0%)] transition-[clip-path]
    duration-300 ease-out"
  aria-hidden="true"
/>
```

The parent card must have `group` and `relative overflow-hidden` for this to work. At rest the bar is clipped to zero width (`inset(0 50%)`); on hover it expands to full width (`inset(0 0%)`).

## Exceptions

Not every card follows the standard recipe. These are the documented deviations:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Component</th>
      <th>Deviation</th>
      <th>Reason</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><code>MatchTeaser</code> / <code>MatchResultRow</code></td>

    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>ContactCard</code></td>
      <td>No accent bar</td>
